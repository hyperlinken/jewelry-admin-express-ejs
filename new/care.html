Jewelry Care Tips by Material

Gold Jewelry

  • Store gold separately to prevent scratching.
  • Clean using a mild dish soap and warm water solution.
  • Dry thoroughly with a soft cloth to avoid tarnish spots.
  • Remove before bathing, swimming, or exercising.

Diamond Jewelry

  • Clean diamonds with a soft toothbrush and ammonia-free cleaner.
  • Avoid direct contact with cosmetics or hairspray.
  • Store in a padded box to protect settings and edges.
  • Inspect prongs and clasps regularly for security.

Silver Jewelry

  • Use a silver polish cloth to remove tarnish gently.
  • Avoid water, perfumes, and air exposure when stored.
  • Store in anti-tarnish pouches or airtight bags.
  • Clean regularly to maintain shine and prevent oxidation.

Gemstone Jewelry
